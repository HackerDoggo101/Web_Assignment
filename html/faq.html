FAQs

Can I add a greeting card to my order?

A complimentary greeting card is given when you purchase a hamper. There is a limit of 25 words. Please type your message in the “Order Notes / Message” section during checkout.

What should I do if I receive a defect / wrong item(s) with my order?

Please check all your items upon collection / delivery. Please inform our staff at the time of collection if there are any defects or discrepancies.

Can I cancel my order?

Goods sold are not returnable or exchangeable.

Franchising

Thank you for your interest, we are not offering any franchises currently.

Should I refrigerate my product upon collection / delivery?

Our products contain no added preservatives. Please refer to product labels for storage temperature and handling instructions.

Can I bring cake lapis or any of your products overseas?

We do not recommend bringing our lapis or any chilled items overseas. It is because our products do not contain any added preservatives. Any temperature fluctuations in handling and storage will compromise its shelf-life and quality.
