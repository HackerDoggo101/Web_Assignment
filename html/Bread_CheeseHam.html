Cheese Ham

RM4
FLOUR : 7/10
SUGAR : 5/10
TASTE : 8/10

Cheese ham bread is a delicious and savory dish that combines three of the most beloved foods: bread, cheese, and ham. It is a classic recipe that has been enjoyed by people all over the world for many years.

The base of the dish is a fresh loaf of bread, which can be either white or whole wheat, depending on your preference. The bread is sliced and then layered with slices of high-quality ham, such as prosciutto or black forest ham. The ham adds a salty and savory flavor to the dish, which perfectly complements the other ingredients.

Next, a generous amount of grated cheese is sprinkled over the top of the ham. The type of cheese used can vary depending on your taste and availability, but some popular options include cheddar, Swiss, or Gouda. The cheese adds a creamy and tangy flavor to the bread, and also helps to bind the ham and bread together.

Once the ham and cheese are layered on the bread, the whole thing is baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the bread is toasted and crispy. This step is important because it helps to meld all the flavors together and creates a deliciously gooey and crispy texture.

Cheese ham bread is a versatile dish that can be served in a variety of ways. It can be enjoyed as a main course for lunch or dinner, or cut into smaller pieces and served as an appetizer or snack. It can also be served hot or cold, depending on your preference.

Overall, cheese ham bread is a delicious and comforting dish that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you are looking for a quick and easy lunch, or a hearty and satisfying dinner, cheese ham bread is sure to hit the spot.
