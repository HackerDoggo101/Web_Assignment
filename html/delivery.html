Delivery

Delicious baked goods delivered to your door!

Welcome to our bakery shop delivery page! We offer delivery services for all of our baked goods, including cakes, pastries, breads, and more.

Delivery Information

  • We deliver within a 10-mile radius of our bakery shop. Delivery is available Monday-Saturday from 9am-5pm.
  • Orders must be placed at least 24 hours in advance.
  • If you prefer to visit the nearest shop to pick up your purchases, you may also select the pickup option.
  • Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Please contact us for more information.
