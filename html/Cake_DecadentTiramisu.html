DECADENT TIRAMUSU CAKE

RM5
FLOUR : 6/10
SUGAR : 8/10
TASTE : 9/10

Decadent Tiramisu Cake is a delicious dessert that combines the classic flavors of tiramisu with the richness and decadence of a cake. This cake is the perfect choice for anyone who loves the taste of tiramisu but wants something a little more substantial and satisfying. The cake consists of layers of espresso-soaked sponge cake, fluffy and creamy mascarpone filling, and a dusting of cocoa powder on top.

The sponge cake is made using eggs, sugar, flour, and a shot of strong espresso, which gives it a deep and intense flavor. The cake is then cut into thin layers and soaked in more espresso, which not only intensifies the coffee flavor but also ensures that the cake stays moist and tender. The mascarpone filling is made using creamy mascarpone cheese, whipped cream, and a touch of sugar. The filling is light and fluffy, with a delicate sweetness that complements the bitterness of the coffee perfectly.

Once the cake layers and filling are assembled, the entire cake is chilled in the refrigerator, allowing the flavors to meld together and the filling to set. Just before serving, the cake is dusted with a generous amount of cocoa powder, which not only adds a touch of elegance to the presentation but also enhances the richness of the cake.

The result is a truly decadent dessert that is perfect for special occasions, dinner parties, or any time you want to indulge in something truly delicious. The combination of rich coffee flavor, creamy mascarpone filling, and tender sponge cake make this dessert a true standout, and it's sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

In conclusion, Decadent Tiramisu Cake is a must-try for anyone who loves the classic Italian dessert of tiramisu. With its rich and intense coffee flavor, light and fluffy filling, and tender sponge cake layers, this dessert is sure to become a favorite among anyone who tries it. So if you're looking for a truly indulgent and satisfying dessert, look no further than Decadent Tiramisu Cake.
